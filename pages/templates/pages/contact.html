How to Report an Outbreak

When to Report

Report immediately if you observe any of the following:

  • Sudden onset of high fever in multiple animals
  • Excessive salivation or drooling
  • Blisters or lesions on tongue, mouth, feet, or teats
  • Sudden lameness affecting multiple animals
  • Dramatic drop in milk production
  • Any unusual illness spreading through your herd

What Information to Provide

  1. Your name and contact information
  2. Farm location and GPS coordinates if available
  3. Number and types of animals affected
  4. Symptoms observed and when they started
  5. Recent animal movements or visitors to the farm
  6. Current location of affected animals
