FMD Control Measures

Guidelines for farmers and livestock owners

If You Suspect FMD

Immediate action is critical. Do not wait to see if symptoms worsen.

Contact Immediately:

Veterinary Services: +268 2404 2731

Emergency Hotline: +268 7600 0000

Prevention Measures

1. Biosecurity on Your Farm

  • Control and record all visitors to your farm
  • Provide disinfectant footbaths at farm entrances
  • Clean and disinfect all vehicles entering the farm
  • Maintain separate equipment for different animal groups
  • Keep wild animals away from domestic livestock
  • Store feed in protected areas to prevent contamination

2. Animal Movement Control

  • Minimize livestock movements during outbreak periods
  • Always obtain proper movement permits when required
  • Quarantine all new animals for at least 21 days
  • Keep detailed records of all animal movements
  • Avoid communal grazing areas during outbreaks
  • Do not mix animals from different farms

3. Vaccination Programs

  • Follow government vaccination schedules strictly
  • Ensure all eligible animals are vaccinated
  • Keep vaccination records up to date
  • Report any adverse reactions to veterinary services
  • Participate in national vaccination campaigns

4. Early Detection

  • Inspect animals daily for any signs of illness
  • Know the symptoms of FMD (see signs below)
  • Report any unusual symptoms immediately
  • Monitor body temperature regularly
  • Watch for sudden drops in milk production

Recognizing FMD Signs

Early Warning Signs

  • High fever (39-41Â°C)
  • Loss of appetite
  • Excessive drooling
  • Smacking lips
  • Shivering

Advanced Symptoms

  • Blisters on tongue and mouth
  • Lesions on hooves and teats
  • Severe lameness
  • Drop in milk production
  • Weight loss

If FMD is Confirmed on Your Farm

1

Isolate Affected Animals

Separate sick animals from healthy ones immediately. Keep different age groups apart.

2

Stop All Movements

Do not move any animals, vehicles, or equipment on or off your farm without permission.

3

Cooperate with Authorities

Follow all instructions from veterinary officials. Provide accurate information and full access.

4

Enhanced Biosecurity

Implement strict cleaning and disinfection protocols. Use approved disinfectants for FMD control.

5

Support Treatment

Provide supportive care as directed: soft food, water, shelter. Do not use unprescribed treatments.
